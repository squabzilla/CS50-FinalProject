Fighters

There's more to me then hitting stuff, you know.

Overview

Fighters are the most common type of adventurer, covering themselves in whatever armor they can find or afford, using a weapon to kill their foe. Fighters are often looked down on, simply because the bar to claim "I'm a figher" is incredibly low, and many wanna-be adventurers are just someone who picked up a club and declared themsef an adventurer. However, be wary of underestimating them - the best of them can defeat a rampaging minotaur without even drawing their sword.
